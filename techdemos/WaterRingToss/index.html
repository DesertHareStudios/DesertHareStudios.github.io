Water Ring Toss

Toss rings in water!

Use streams of bubbles and gravity to insert plastic rings in the poles!

This is a tech demo meant to simulate your childhood toy as close as possible and be a testing ground for new tech that we may use in other games.

Features:

  • Simple, fast and kinda realistic fluid and aerodynamics simulation
  • Dynamic resolution and PC-like quality customization
  • Day and night toggle
  • Physical camera lens and exposure
